Overview

Carbon materials that line and power furnaces

Vigoraque supplies carbon blocks and paste products that protect and operate high-temperature furnaces. Carbon and cathode blocks form durable, thermally conductive linings, while ramming and electrode pastes provide monolithic joints and self-baking electrodes for smelting operations.

Blocks are produced to custom dimensions, and pastes are formulated for the working temperature and duty of your process — from blast furnaces to submerged-arc and reduction cells.

  • High thermal conductivityEfficient heat transfer in furnace hearths.
  • Chemical & slag resistanceDurable in aggressive molten environments.
  • Custom block sizesCut and shaped to your lining design.
  • Self-baking paste optionsRamming and Söderberg electrode pastes.
