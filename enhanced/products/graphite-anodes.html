Graphite Anodes

Corrosion-resistant anodes with stable conductivity for electroplating, electrolysis and chemical processing.

Overview

Reliable conduction in aggressive media

Vigoraque graphite anodes deliver stable electrical conductivity and outstanding corrosion resistance in electrochemical environments. Their inert carbon structure withstands acids, alkalis and chlorine, making them a long-service alternative to metal anodes in many processes.

Supplied as plates, rods and blocks — and machined to custom geometries — they support electroplating lines, electrolysis cells and a range of chemical and electrowinning applications.

  • High corrosion resistanceStable in acidic, alkaline and chlorine media.
  • Consistent conductivityLow, stable resistance across service life.
  • Custom shapesPlates, rods and blocks machined to drawing.
  • Long service lifeLower replacement frequency and downtime.
