Company

Two decades of graphite manufacturing from Shanghai

Vigoraque is the international brand of Shanghai Shen-Tech Graphite Material Co., Ltd. Founded in 2001, we manufacture and export a wide range of carbon and graphite products — with graphite electrodes at the core of our steelmaking, metallurgy and chemical customers' operations.

As a direct manufacturer and exporter, we control quality from raw material through baking, impregnation, graphitization and precision machining — and ship to partners across Europe, Africa, the Americas and Asia.
