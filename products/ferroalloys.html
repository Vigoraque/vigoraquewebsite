Overview

Alloying and deoxidation agents for modern steelmaking

Vigoraque supplies a broad range of ferroalloys, including ferrosilicon (FeSi), ferromanganese (FeMn) and silicomanganese (SiMn), used to deoxidize, alloy and adjust the chemistry of steel and cast iron. Each grade is sourced from our parent group's trusted manufacturing network and matched to your furnace practice and specification.

Supplied in lump, granulated or milled form, our ferroalloys support EAF, BOF and foundry operations that demand consistent chemistry, low trace elements and reliable delivery.

  • FeSi · FeMn · SiMn gradesMatched to your alloy specification.
  • Consistent chemistryTight compositional control, batch to batch.
  • Flexible sizingLump, granulated or milled, to your process.
  • Reliable deliveryReal-time pricing and logistics across 3 continents.
