Overview

The core of electric-arc steelmaking

Vigoraque supplies UHP, HP and RP graphite electrodes that conduct the high currents used to melt scrap and refine steel in electric-arc (EAF) and ladle furnaces. Sourced from our parent group's trusted manufacturing network, each electrode is produced from premium needle coke and supplied with matched nipple connections.

Available across a full diameter range, they are selected for high oxidation resistance, low specific resistance and reliable thermal-mechanical performance under demanding furnace cycles.

  • UHP / HP / RP gradesMatched to your furnace power and duty cycle.
  • Low specific resistanceEfficient current transfer, lower energy loss.
  • Precision-machined nipplesSecure joints and stable arc operation.
  • Reliable global deliveryReal-time pricing and logistics across 3 continents.
