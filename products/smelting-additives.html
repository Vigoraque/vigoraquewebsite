Overview

Carbon materials that line, charge and power furnaces

Vigoraque supplies a full range of carbon-based additives that support high-temperature steel and metal smelting. Carbon and cathode blocks form durable, thermally conductive furnace linings, calcined carbon additives and recarburizers adjust melt chemistry, and ramming/electrode pastes provide monolithic joints and self-baking electrodes for smelting operations.

Every additive is sourced from our trusted manufacturing network and sized or formulated for the working temperature and duty of your process, from EAF and LF steelmaking to submerged-arc and reduction cells.

  • Carbon & recarburizer additivesPrecise carbon pickup for melt chemistry control.
  • Chemical & slag resistanceDurable in aggressive molten environments.
  • Custom block sizesCut and shaped to your lining design.
  • Self-baking paste optionsRamming and Söderberg electrode pastes.
