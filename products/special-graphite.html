Special Graphite

Fine-grain, high-purity graphite machined to tight tolerances for EDM tooling and high-temperature applications.

Overview

Precision carbon for demanding tooling

Vigoraque special graphite is a fine-grain, isostatically pressed and molded material engineered for uniform structure and excellent machinability. Its combination of high purity, thermal shock resistance and dimensional stability makes it ideal for electrical-discharge machining (EDM) and high-temperature fixtures.

We machine special graphite to customer drawings — from EDM electrodes and casting dies to crucibles and heat-zone components used in furnaces and crystal growth.

  • Fine, uniform grainClean edges and detailed geometries when machined.
  • High purityLow ash grades for sensitive processes.
  • Thermal shock resistanceStable through rapid heating and cooling.
  • Machined to drawingCustom components to tight tolerances.
